I envisioned shared gardens within cities where anyone in the community can plant and grow food freely, for everyone’s benefit, with the support of solar-powered robots. Spaces to gather, connect, and spend some relaxing time together, where workshops for children and other community activities can also be organized. A service by the community, for the community.
